---
title: Claim Channel
description: Take ownership of a channel whose owner has left.
icon: Download
---


Use `/claim` to become the new owner of a temporary channel when the original owner is no longer in the channel. This is useful when a channel stays active after the owner disconnected.

<Callout title="Note" type="info">
    You can only claim a channel if the current owner is not present. If the owner is still inside, the claim will be rejected.
</Callout>
