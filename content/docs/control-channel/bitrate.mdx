---
title: Channel Bitrate
description: Adjust the audio bitrate of your temporary channel.
icon: Activity
---

Use `/bitrate` to set the audio quality of your channel. The value is specified in kbps and must be between ``8`` and your server's maximum bitrate (up to ``384`` kbps).

<Callout title="Note" type="idea">
    This overwrites the default Temporary Channel Bitrate configured in the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.horizon-bot.me) if the user saves the channel to a
    ``Session profile``!
</Callout>
