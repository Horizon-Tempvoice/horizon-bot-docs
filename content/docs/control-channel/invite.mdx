---
title: Invite User
description: Invite a user to your temporary channel.
icon: Mail
---


Use `/inviteuser` to open a user picker and send a selected member a direct message invite. The invited user is automatically trusted, so they can join even if your channel is locked or hidden.

<Callout title="Note" type="idea">
    Invited users receive a DM notification. They can opt out of these notifications at any time by clicking the button in the DM.
</Callout>
