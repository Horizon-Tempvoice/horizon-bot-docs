---
title: Kick User
description: Disconnect a user from your temporary channel.
icon: DoorOpen
---


Use `/kick @user` to immediately disconnect a member from your channel. They are not blocked and can rejoin unless the channel is locked or hidden.

<Callout title="Note" type="info">
    You cannot kick yourself or the channel owner. Some members may also be protected by server admins and cannot be kicked (e.g. server moderators).
</Callout>

<Callout title="Hint" type="info">
    To prevent the user from rejoining, you can use the [/lock](/control-channel/lock) command or block the user with the [/block](/control-channel/block) command.
</Callout>
