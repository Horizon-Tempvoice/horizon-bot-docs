---
title: Autosave
description: Automatically update your active session when you leave your channel.
icon: RefreshCw
---

Use `/session autosave [enabled]` to turn autosave on or off. When enabled, your active session slot is automatically updated with your current channel state every time you leave.

<Callout title="Note" type="info">
    Autosave requires an active session slot. Use `/session activate` first.
</Callout>

<Callout title="When autosave is skipped" type="warn">
    Your channel state will **not** be saved automatically if:
    - You have no active session slot set
    - The server admin has disabled the session feature
    - The channel was transferred to you (you are not the original creator)
    - You claimed the channel from an owner who left

    You can still save manually with `/session save`, but be careful: this will overwrite your profile with the current channel state.
</Callout>
