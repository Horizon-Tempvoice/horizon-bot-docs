---
title: "{RANDOM}"
description: Inserts a randomly selected word from a predefined list into your channel name.
---

Each time a channel is created, `{RANDOM}` picks one word at random from a list you define and inserts it into the channel name.

With the words ``Warrior``, ``Mage``, and ``Rogue`` configured, a template like `` ⚔️・{RANDOM} `` could produce:

- ``⚔️・Warrior``
- ``⚔️・Mage``
- ``⚔️・Rogue``

<Callout title="Customize the word list" type="idea">
    Click the ``{ }`` button to open the placeholder overview, then use the sliders icon next to ``{RANDOM}`` to change the predefined words.
</Callout>
