---
title: "{OWNER_USERNAME}"
description: Inserts the Discord account username of the channel owner.
---

`{OWNER_USERNAME}` is replaced by the Discord account username of the person who created the channel. This is the unique handle tied to their account, not their server-specific display name.

A template like `` 🎙・{OWNER_USERNAME} `` would produce channels such as:

- ``🎙・john_doe``
- ``🎙・gamer123``
- ``🎙・fabi``
